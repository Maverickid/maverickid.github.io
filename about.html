Ever woke up late on a Saturday morning, made a sandwich in your pyjamas and thought to your self, "This has to be the best fucking sandwich ever made in the world" ? Well, that is exactly what I did on Jan 6th, 2024. With barely opened eyes I put together 2 garlic breads and stuffed in between it a Tyson chicken patty, a smear of mayonnaise and BBQ sauce, a thin slice of onion and tomato and exactly one leaf of lettuce. And lo and behold, on the first bite of that sandwich, my eyes widened and I had one of those really rare moments people have in life, where you know your life is not going to be the same again.


But the question still lingered...was it the best or could a better sandwich exist in the tangible confines of the planet we inhabit, that you and I are not aware of? And of course, I know what you are thinking.."it is all subjective, kiddo". But that very thought is what I wanted to explore.


So going with my gut(pun intended) and having recently learnt what HTML stands for, I embarked on this project where we each can build our own sandwiches, take a poll and see if our collective conciousness would converge on a clear winner.


Thus was born 'The Deli Deli-cious Project'. If you think you have a winner in mind, I urge you to sumbit your sandwich stack. We will tally the votes at the end of the year and will declare the results on my instagram account.


I have made the final sandwich render a shareable image of how you like your sandwich, incase your room-mates were wondering. *wink* x 2
